01. Housing Dissassembly

Primary objective: Access the internal seal chamber without compromising the housing structure.

Difficulty 3/5

Using the 14mm socket wrench, loosen the eight perimeter bolts in a "star pattern" to ensure even pressure release. Do not fully remove any single bolt before all have been cracked open.

Once tension is relieved, hand-turn the remaining threads and store bolts in a magnetic tray. The housing cover may require a light tap with a rubber mallet to break the factory vacuum seal.

Foreman Tip: If the cover remains stuck, do not use a flathead screwdriver to pry it open, as this will gouge the mating surface and cause future leaks.
